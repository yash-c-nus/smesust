Methodology Notes

📅

Data Window

Most indicators request 3 years of historical data to capture trends and direction of travel, not just a single snapshot.

🔀

Cross-Pillar Indicators

Some governance questions (Q4.6, Q4.7, Q4.9) are mapped to Carbon & Climate Performance for thematic coherence.

📝

Self-Reported Data

The scorecard is based on company-reported data. Verification and assurance levels are noted where applicable.

🏢

SME Focus

Questions are calibrated for small and medium enterprises — recognising different resource levels and reporting maturity.
